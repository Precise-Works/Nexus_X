NEXUS X SPEAKERS

Meet The Visionaries

Franklin Gbaiwogha

Professional Speaker • Brand Strategist • Investor

Franklin Gbaiwogha is a Professional Speaker, Brand Strategist and Investor. He's the Creative Director at The Learning Embassy, Nigeria; a leadership and capacity development organization helping individuals and brands attain financial success.

He is also the C.E.O. of TLE Properties and has trained thousands on Business, Leadership, and Communication across Africa.

Franklin currently serves as the South-South regional coordinator of the Nigeria Youth Futures Fund (NYFF), an initiative of LEAP Africa.

Isaac Ben

Blockchain Strategic Marketing Professional

Isaac Ben is a blockchain strategic marketing professional recognized for driving growth, innovation, and ecosystem expansion within the Web3 and blockchain industry.

Widely known as “God Will Provide,” he collaborates with builders, founders, and investors to foster impactful partnerships and accelerate ecosystem development.

With experience in broadcast media, growth management, and brand management, Isaac focuses on connecting ideas, people, and opportunities shaping the decentralized economy.

Emmanuel Umoh

Entrepreneur • Project Management Professional • AI Strategist

Emmanuel Umoh is a Nigerian Canadian based entrepreneur, project management professional, and AI strategist whose journey reflects resilience, innovation, and a commitment to continuous growth.

After relocating from Nigeria to Canada in pursuit of greater opportunities, Emmanuel earned a Master's degree in Project Management from Northeastern University and built a career leading complex projects and helping organizations achieve strategic goals.

Over the years, he has managed multimillion-dollar initiatives, developed expertise in business systems and leadership, and become known for transforming challenges into scalable solutions.

Today, Emmanuel is the founder of Chimney Studios and a successful business owner in Canada, where he helps entrepreneurs and organizations harness the power of artificial intelligence, automation, and strategic systems to accelerate growth and improve efficiency.
